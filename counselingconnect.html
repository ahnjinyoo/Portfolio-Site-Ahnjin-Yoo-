CounselingConnect - Therapy app for the modern age

Starting a product design course in a pandemic made me want to create an app idea that benefits people during these turbulent times. It goes without saying that during these times, people have become burned out, and mental health has become worse and worse. Unfortunately, meeting a counselor is difficult these days due to restrictions, and many of the counseling apps available do not have a system in place where people can get to know the counselor before signing up. Therefore, I wanted to develop an app that people can get to know counselors beforehand and connect with counselors without the pressure of signing up.
