CounselingConnect - Therapy app for the modern age

Starting a product design course in a pandemic made me want to create an app idea that benefits people during these turbulent times. It goes without saying that during these times, people have become burned out, and mental health has become worse and worse. Unfortunately, meeting a counselor is difficult these days due to restrictions, and many of the counseling apps available do not have a system in place where people can get to know the counselor before signing up. Therefore, I wanted to develop an app that people can get to know counselors beforehand and connect with counselors without the pressure of signing up. I was inspired by how influential social media has become in our society and applied that idea to a counseling app. CounselingConnect is an app where people can get to know counselors and therapists on a deeper level while working through one‚Äôs problems and issues.

Domus - Swipe right to find your dream home!

Domus is a quick and easy way of finding the dream house for users. With an intuitive swiping system for users, they can easily navigate to find their dream house. While there are plenty of blogs and agencies providing information, often, buyers new to the market may struggle to get started without professional guidance and waste time viewing properties out of their range. Domus provides them with the expertise needed to get started efficiently.
